Tempo for macOS

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

Tempo does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything the app stores stays entirely on your device.

This policy explains what data Tempo stores locally and how it is used. There are no analytics, no tracking, no servers, and no third-party SDKs that receive your information.

Data Stored on Your Device

Tempo stores the following data locally using macOS system storage (UserDefaults). This data never leaves your Mac.

⚙️
Settings & Preferences
Timer durations, session names, colors, icons, sounds, and display options you configure in the app.
📋
Tasks
Task names, notes, due dates, and completion status that you enter in the task list.
📊
Session History
A local log of completed focus and break sessions, used to calculate streaks and daily progress. Capped at 100 records.
🎮
Game Stats
Win/loss counts and high scores for the built-in mini-games (2048, Minesweeper, Tic Tac Toe, Memory, Theseus, Nonogram).

What We Don't Collect

Tempo never accesses or collects:

🚫
No personal information never
No name, email, Apple ID, or account of any kind is required or collected.
🚫
No usage analytics never
No analytics SDK, crash reporter, or usage tracking is included in the app.
🚫
No network requests never
Tempo makes no network connections. It functions entirely offline.
🚫
No third-party sharing never
No data is shared with advertisers, partners, or any third party.

Notifications

Tempo requests permission to send macOS notifications to alert you when a focus session or break ends. This permission is optional — the app is fully functional without it.

Notifications are generated locally on your device. No notification content is transmitted externally.

Accessibility Permission

The optional global keyboard shortcut (⌘⇧P) requires macOS Accessibility permission. This permission is used solely to listen for that key combination system-wide. Tempo does not read, record, or transmit any other keyboard input or screen content.

This permission is entirely optional. You can use Tempo without granting it.

Deleting Your Data

You can reset all session and game stats at any time from Settings → Stats → Reset All Stats.

Uninstalling Tempo removes all locally stored data from your Mac.

Children's Privacy

Tempo does not collect any data from any user, including children. The app complies with COPPA and applicable children's privacy laws.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date. Because no data is collected, changes are unlikely to affect your privacy in any material way.
